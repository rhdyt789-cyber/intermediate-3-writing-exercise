Nowadays, many teenagers have got video consoles at home and they often like playing active video games.

First of all, I think that active video games are a good way to keep fit. There are lots of different types of exercise you can do and you can play them in the comfort of your own home.

In addition, you can play them whenever you want. Some people think that it's better to do exercise outside in the fresh air. Although this is true, active video games are a useful alternative when the weather is bad.

To sum up, I believe that video games are a fun and social way to keep fit.

Part A: Reading Comprehension

1. Why does the writer think active video games are good?
2. Name one activity mentioned in the essay.
3. What is another advantage of active video games?
4. What alternative viewpoint is mentioned?
5. Does the writer support active video games?
